My first blogpost is dedicated to my dad and the story of how he kicked an unleashed dog away from my toddler brother at the park. I wasn't there, but I imagine it being a small frail dog that squeaked when it hit my dad's shoe and sailed over the owner's head. 

It reminds me of how Hana whacks around Boss with her pickleball paddle and he still loves her. Which then reminds me of the tragic passing of Colin's unloved dogs. To me these exemplify the finitude of love.
