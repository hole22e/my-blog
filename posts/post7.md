"What are Emojis?
Emojis look like images, but they are not."
(W3schools.com)

Watched Hoppers with Allison at 12 in the midday and it was a thriller a seat-edge gripper. Political/environmental messaging was weak but the true significance of the movie is revealed about three quarters in when the animals use emojis to communicate with Jerry. They control him emotionally, psychologically with just a few <a href="/posts/post9/">well-placed emojis</a>.
